# Kethematics - The Study of Family Dynamics (Kethematics)

**Kethematics** is a newly proposed field of study that explores the role of family in human history and its profound impact on individuals, society, and culture. In the era of Artificial Intelligence (AI), the role of family as the core unit of socialization needs to be re-examined. Kethematics aims to study the influence of family from interdisciplinary perspectives, drawing from history, culture, psychology, sociology, and emerging technologies like AI.

Kethematics is not just an intersection of psychology and sociology, but an independent field of study focused on **how family impacts individuals and society**. Key areas of research include:

- **Family Structure**: Study of family roles and functions, and how families respond to societal changes.
- **Family Education**: Exploration of how family influences the development of children, particularly in the context of changing educational systems.
- **Intergenerational Transmission**: How cultural values, behaviors, and social patterns are passed down through generations.
- **Societal and Cultural Contexts**: Investigating the differences in family structures and roles across various cultural and social contexts, with a special focus on modern transformations.
